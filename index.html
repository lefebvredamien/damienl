About Me

About Me

Born and raised in the French Alps, I grew up passionate about art and technology. I started programming in Basic and taking piano lessons in 6th grade and have never stopped learning about technology, music, and all the other arts ever since.

After finishing the Lycee I flew halfway across the globe to study mathematics at the University of California, Los Angeles. There I developed a whole new appreciation for design in such a vibrant city, and became a skilled data scientist through coursework and internships.

At 21 years old I graduated and left for New York City, where I started working in FinTech. The fast-pace of the East Coast taught me a new spin on how to get things done with passion. In 2019 my older brother, Val Lefebvre, launched a startup Silver Moon at the intersection of art and technology, and I've been collaborating with him on this journey ever since.
